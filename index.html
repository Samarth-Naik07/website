About the Book

An Author's Note: The Road to The Last Aetherium

Writing The Last Aetherium has been a journey almost as epic as the one Kaelen takes.


  • The idea first came to me on a cold, rainy evening, where I found myself
    pondering the nature of betrayal and redemption. I wanted to build a world that
    felt both familiar and utterly new, a place where a hero wasn't born perfect but was
    forged through hardship and painful choices. Every character, every magical relic, and
    every scar on the world's surface was created with the hope of building a story that would
    resonate with the epic fantasy fans I've always admired.
